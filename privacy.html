Privacy Policy for My Easy Reply

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application My Easy Reply (“Application”) for mobile devices, created by Choo’s Tools Apps. The Application is a utility tool designed to help users manage and respond to messages efficiently using screen overlays and automation features.

1. Accessibility Service API Usage (Important Disclosure)

My Easy Reply uses the Android AccessibilityServiceAPI to provide its core functionality.

  • How we use it: We use this service to detect when you receive notifications from messaging apps and to display a "Quick Reply" button as an overlay on your screen.
  • Data Collection: We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any data processed through the Accessibility Service. We do not monitor your private conversations, keystrokes, passwords, or financial information.
  • User Consent: The use of this service is entirely optional. However, the overlay reply feature will not function without it. You must explicitly grant permission via the "Prominent Disclosure" screen within the app and the Android System Settings.

2. Information Collection and Use

A. Personal Information: We do not require account registration. We do not collect "Personal Information" such as your name, email address, or phone number.

B. Local Data: All messages you create or save in the Application are stored locally on your device using a Room Database. We have no access to this data.
