# Privacy Policy / Dasar Privasi / 隐私政策 **Last Updated 30 June 2026 / Dikemas kini 30 Jun 2026/ 更新日期:30 June 2026 --- ## 🇺🇸 English ### 1. Personal Data Collection We do not directly collect, capture, or store personal identifiers, contact details, financial transactions, or data entry history logged inside the application. Any information you input into the calculator calculations remains local to your device runtime memory. ### 2. Third-Party Services & Data Processing This app integrates third-party Software Development Kits (SDKs) that automatically collect certain device configurations and tracking information to serve, optimize, and secure promotional materials. These automatic background data transfers are managed by external services. You can review the privacy guidelines, tracking parameters, and conditions of these external service providers via their official links below: * [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) * [Google AdMob Privacy Policy & Partner Terms](https://developers.google.com/admob/android/privacy) Data collected by these networks may include device advertising identifiers (e.g., AAID/GAID), IP addresses, and anonymous app performance diagnostics. ### 3. Device Identifiers & User Control Integrated SDKs via Google AdMob use device advertising identifiers to optimize performance and compile anonymous metric tracking. You can view, reset, or completely opt out of personalized tracking metrics at any time inside your Android device's system settings under **Settings -> Privacy -> Ads**. --- ## 🇲🇾 Bahasa Melayu ### 1.
